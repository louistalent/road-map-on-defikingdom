DragonBlood

Dragon is the oldest ancestor left on this list. There is only one way a heritage this old is still alive and that’s to be completely dominant on the battlefield.
Dragons are the most powerful, wealthy, and confident creatures to ever roam this Earth.
They have the strength to snag and carry and enemies off the battlefield, but at the same time resort to their strategic tactics more often than not by breathing fire from a distance.
Much like their ancestors, the DragonBloods wield an astonishing amount of strength.
Their attacks are cause tremendous damage so proceed to attack Dragonbloods carefully.
Along with their increased total damage, DragonBloods have the power of fire running through their veins.
This allows DragonBloods to call on their ancestors during battle and have a unique ticking fire damage on their attacks. However, the arrogance of the dragon is often it’s downfall.
Critical damage resistance and damage resistance is decreased due to the tendency of DragonBloods being too arrogant on the battlefield

BearBlood

Bear is a unique ancestor that has survived the tells of time.
The reason behind it is simple.
Bears are the most courageous, spiritual, and instinctive creatures on this planet.
Bears wield the power to rip apart most land animals, however they only do it when their instincts kick in.
And when it does kick in, Bears will stop at nothing to make sure them and their cubs are safe.
BearBloods have this same warrior instincts instilled in their blood.
They fear nothing and trust that their instincts and courage will carry them to victory.
The strong connection to their spirit has carried them this far into the war so why would it fail them now? Due to their connection with the spirits and their own instincts, bearbloods are born with an increased number of stamina and health.
They can summon their inner spirit for increased critical damage and increased total damage.
However, Bears are the slowest bloodline left, so they do lack more attack speed than others.
They also have lower magic resistance since their instincts and spirits

WolfBlood

The wolf is the smallest, least dominant animal on this list.
By themselves at least.
Wolves have a unique way of surviving.
They live in pacts.
Pacts that create a bond where each wolf is willing to risk his life for the other or the greater good of the pact.
This loyalty mixed with their life-or-death mentality is a combination that is destined for great strength.
Add in the fact that they know when to cut their losses and retreat, then you can see while they lasted this long.
Wolfbloods carry this same mentality with them, they travel and fight for their pact and do not make decisions that will do more harm than good.
Wolves are fast and swift which is why Wolfbloods get an increased amount of attack speed, and wolves can call on their pact which increases defense, gains health, and increases attack damage 3x.
Since wolves try to be quick and easy, they have less damage resistance, possess less attack damage per attack, and they have less total health.

LionBlood

Lions are arguably the most balanced warrior to ever touch Earth.
Lions possess the strength to go head-to-head with any animal, along with grace and finesse to chase any animal down in any terrain.
Lions are just absolute killing machines and control the Sahara.
As if a tiger’s physical abilities don’t present a big enough problem by themselves, tigers approach every encounter with respect.
This allows for the lions to scale their surroundings carefully and wait for the perfect time to attack.
This makes lions the most lethal animal on this planet.
The LionBloods took this mentality from their ancestors and brought it to war.
LionBloods are extremely balanced in warfare, not too slow, not too weak, nor do they enter a situation without preparation.
LionBloods gain boosts in damage, stamina, intelligence, and total health.
They also call onto their ancestors to give them a massive boost in attack speed and critical chance hit.
However, Lionbloods have the lowest amount of magic and are vulnerable to other magic attacks, along with have lower damage resistance.

SharkBlood

Sharks are one of the most feared predators to ever exist.
Sharks have three things that make them stand out.
The power they have, the authority they have, the independence they have.
The power of sharks is what makes them so feared, they are the rulers of the ocean.
Along with a bite and razor-sharp teeth that can tear through anything in its path.
Sharks can roam the ocean alone and still be victorious.
Sharks fear none, but everything fears Sharks.
They have controlled their domain, and answer to no one.
Sharks completely rule the ocean.
Much is why Sharkbloods answer to no one on Earth as well.
They have lived for this moment.
The moment to prove that they have always been the best warrior.
Sharkbloods gain an increase in power and does more damage once armor is removed.
They also gain a bleeding damage over time affect from their ancestor’s razor-sharp teeth.
They also have good fire resistance, but their damage resistance falls lower on the ranks, along with their total stamina.
